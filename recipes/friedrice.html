Friedrice


Description

Fried rice is a popular Asian dish made from pre-cooked rice that is stir-fried in a wok or a frying pan with ingredients such as vegetables, eggs, and meat. It is often seasoned with soy sauce, garlic, and other spices to enhance its flavor.

Ingredients

  • ⅔ cup chopped baby carrots
  • ½ cup frozen green peas
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced, or to taste (Optional)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups leftover cooked and chilled white rice
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce, or more to taste
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil, or to taste

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the vegetable oil and swirl to coat the pan.
  2. Add the chopped carrots and frozen peas. Stir-fry for about 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  3. Add the minced garlic (if using) and stir-fry for another 30 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Push the vegetables to one side of the pan. Crack the eggs into the empty side and scramble them until fully cooked.
  5. Add the chilled rice to the pan, breaking up any clumps. Stir everything together until well combined.
  6. Pour in the soy sauce and sesame oil, mixing thoroughly to ensure even distribution.
  7. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, stirring frequently, until the rice is heated through.
  8. Adjust seasoning with more soy sauce or sesame oil if desired. Serve hot.
