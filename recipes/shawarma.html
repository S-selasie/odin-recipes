Shawarma


Description

Shawarma is a Middle Eastern dish consisting of meat that is cooked on a vertical rotisserie. It is typically served in a wrap or pita with vegetables and sauces.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken or lamb, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground paprika
  • Â½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Pita bread or flatbreads for serving
  • Fresh vegetables (lettuce, tomatoes, cucumbers)
  • Tahini sauce or garlic sauce for drizzling

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix the olive oil, garlic, cumin, paprika, cinnamon, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the sliced meat to the marinade and let it sit for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and cook the marinated meat until fully cooked and slightly crispy.
  4. Warm the pita bread or flatbreads in a separate pan or microwave.
  5. Assemble the shawarma by placing the cooked meat on the bread, adding fresh vegetables, and drizzling with tahini or garlic sauce.
  6. Wrap it up and serve hot.
